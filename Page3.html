Herbal Raw Sugar Scrub
1 cup raw sugar
1/4 cup light oil such as almond, walnut, or sunflower
1 Tbs dried herbs such as lavender, rosemary, thyme, chamomile, or mint

Mix all ingrediants and stir well. Use: Massage a Tbs or two all over your
body to gently exfoliate and moisturize your skin. Follow with warm shower
or bath.

Blueberry Face Mask
2 Tbs fresh or frozen blueberries
1 Tbs plain yogurt
1 Tbs lemon juice

Mash or blend all ingrediants. Use: After cleansing , spread entire mixture
onto face and neck. Leave on for 15 minutes. Rinse and pat dry.
Helps rejuvinate irritated skin and give it a healthy glow.

Cleanser for Acne
1/4 cup Witchhazel
1 Tbs Aloe Vera Gel
10 drops Tea Tree Oil

Mix all ingrediants. Use: Apply cleanser with a cotton ball to face and neck.
Use 2-3 times a day as needed. Tea Tree Oil helps reduce acne , the
Witchhazel is toning and the Aloe Vera Gel is cooling and tightening.

Mint Vinegar Toner
3 Tbs fresh mint or 1 Tbsp dried
2 Tbs Apple Cider Vinegar
1 cup distilled H2o

Mix all together and pour into jar. Let steep for three days, shaking it daily.
Strain and pour into another clean jar with tight fitting lid. Use: Apply over
face and neck with a cotton ball.

Aftershave Gel for Men
1/2 cup Aloe Vera Gel
1 1/2 Tbs H2o

10 drops essential oil of choice such as sandlewood, cedarwood, patchouli, or
sage.

Combine all ingrediants in the container you'll store it in until mixed well.
Top with a tight fighting lid. Use:Pat on skin after every shave to soothe and

Babies Massage Oil

6-8 0z Almond oil

Put flowers in a mason jar 3/4 full and cover completely with oil. Let steep

Sore Muscle & Joint Rub
3-4 medium sized cayenne peppers
2 1/2 cups Olive oil or Almond oil

Dice cayenne peppers into small slices. Add peppers to oil and place in a
pot. Simmer gently and evenly for 10 minutes. Let cool. Once cooled,
strain, and pour into a clean glass mason jar. Use: Massage into affected
area.

LAVENDER OINTMENT
4 Tbs olive oil
3 to 4 Tbs beeswax
3 Tbs cocoa butter
1 vitamin e 400
15 drops lavender oil
Combine the olive oil, beeswax, cocoa butter and heat in the top of a
double boiler. Remove from the heat. Add the vitamin E, Lavender oil
and beat well. Pour into little jars and allow the mixture to cool before
covering. Use: Rub on stings, burns and rashes.

Elder Berry Syrup
1 cup dried Elder Berries
2 cups distilled H2o
1/2 cup honey
Simmer the berries for 30 minutes. Mash with a potato mashing about
half way through cooking. Allow mixture to cool to room temperature
then strain through cheesecloth. Stir in honey. Pour into glass jar and
store in the refrigerator
